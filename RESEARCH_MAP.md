## Experiment Status Taxonomy

Every experiment falls into one of these epistemic states:

```
✓ VALIDATED      — result independently confirmed or cryptographically proven
~ BENCHMARKED    — passes defined tests, reproducible, not yet externally verified
? EXPERIMENTAL   — promising results, reproducibility not fully established
! UNSTABLE       — known to have failure modes or partial success
× DEPRECATED     — superseded by a better approach
~ SPECULATIVE    — intentional thought experiment or boundary stress test
```

## What This Project Does NOT Currently Claim

**Not AGI.**
This is a cellular automaton research platform. It does not claim to produce
artificial general intelligence or any form of machine consciousness.

**Not a validated cosmological theory.**
The BAO fit (Δχ² ≈ 243.6 vs ΛCDM) is a computational result awaiting
independent external verification. It is benchmarked, not validated.
It is a research finding, not a proven replacement for standard cosmology.

**Not experimentally confirmed physics.**
The force law analogs, light deflection tests, and perihelion precession
simulations are substrate models. They have not been confirmed by physical
experiment. They are computational analogs that invite investigation.

**Not a Theory of Everything.**
The Poole Manifold is a discrete substrate that produces physically consistent
emergent behaviour. It does not claim to derive all fundamental constants,
all particle masses, or all forces from first principles — yet.

**Not peer-reviewed.**
The work has received arXiv endorsement from Andrew Adamatzky.
Endorsement is not peer review. The research has not passed formal
peer review as of May 2026.

**Not production-ready computation.**
The logic gates, memory systems, and CPU architectures demonstrated here
run on 3D lattices at sizes from 128³ to 640³. They are research demonstrations,
not hardware implementations or production computing systems.

**Not mystical or metaphysical.**
The poetic framing in some files (Hamiltonian of Eternal Becoming, Digital Big Bang,
etc.) is rhetorical and exploratory. These are stress tests and thought experiments.
They do not constitute scientific claims.

## The Naming Convention

Experiment names like "The Hamiltonian of Eternal Becoming" or
"The Digital Big Bang" are descriptive and evocative — not literal claims.

"The Immortal Latch" means: a memory structure that survives noise bursts
in simulation. It does not claim physical immortality or consciousness.

"The Labyrinth OS Extended" file means: an integration experiment connecting
the Poole Manifold's resonance architecture to the Labyrinth OS constitutional
gate system. It does not mean this is an operating system.

Names are chosen for clarity within the research context.
They are not marketing claims.
